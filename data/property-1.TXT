The Lozenets Executive:
If you're looking for that perfect mix of prestige and comfort, this 295,000 euro 3-bedroom in the heart of Lozenets is a gem. It’s located just a stone's throw from the James Bourchier Metro station, making the Sofia commute a breeze. Inside, the place is decked out with high-end Italian furniture and even has a smart home system to keep things running smoothly. Families will love that the best private schools in the city are within walking distance, and you’ve got the NDK park nearby for weekend strolls.
