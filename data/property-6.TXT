Sarafovo Sea Breeze:
In the quiet Sarafovo district of Burgas, there’s a lovely 2-story house for 215,000 euro. It’s a 3-bedroom property with its own private garden and parking. It’s not furnished, so you can pick your own style. The beach is only about 600 meters away, and you’re close to the airport and the Line 15 bus stop, which makes getting into the main part of Burgas very easy.
